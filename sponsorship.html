Become a sponsor of PyCon Sweden 2016!

The third PyCon Sweden will be a conference with 300 attendees, all sharing the passion for Python programming language. Two days of conference will be filled with world class speakers, both local and international, software engineers, system administrators, specialists, scientists, and other people who rely on the use of Python. The conference provides an unique setting to build connections between organizations and individuals in the Swedish Python scene.

For a sponsor PyCon Sweden 2016 makes it possible to stand out as a Python community supporter, to attract talent, and to make themselves known among other organizations using Python.

For us your support makes it possible for us to keep the participation fee low, enabling the conference to as many people as possible.

Sponsorship options

Gold sponsor (limited to 2)

75 000 SEK

  • Rollup on display in each of the lecture halls, beside the stage for the entire conference.
  • 5 (five) minutes of stage time at the beginning of either the first or second day - first come, first served.
  • Booth at the venue (Guaranteed space)
  • Company logo embodied on the conference bag or lanyard
  • Full-page ad in conference brochure
  • Can get a job ad on the homepage and in the brochure.
  • 5 corporate tickets (transferrable)

Silver sponsor (limited to 10)

35 000 SEK

  • Booth at the venue (first come, first served)
  • Half-page ad in the conference brochure
  • Can get a job ad on the homepage and in the brochure.
  • 2 corporate tickets

Bronze sponsor

10 000 SEK

  • Mention in the conference brochure, with logotype and home page
  • 1 corporate ticket

Other sponsorship options
