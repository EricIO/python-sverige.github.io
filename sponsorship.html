Become a sponsor of PyCon Sweden 2015!

The second PyCon Sweden will be a conference with 250 attendees, all sharing the passion for Python programming language. Two days of conference will be filled with world class speakers, both local and international, software engineers, system administrators, specialists, and other people who rely on the use of Python. Conference provides an unique setting to build connections between organizations and individuals in the Swedish Python scene.

For a sponsor PyCon Sweden 2015 makes it possible to stand out as a Python community supporter, to attract talent, and to make themselves known among other organizations using Python.

For us your support makes it possible for us to keep the participation fee low, enabling the conference to as many people as possible.

Sponsorship options

Gold sponsor (limited to 2)

  • Guaranteed space for booth at the venue
  • 3 corporate tickets
  • Full-page ad in conference brochure
  • Company logo on conference bag or lanyard
  • Get one rollup in each lecture hall

Silver sponsor (limited to 10)

  • Limit of 10 silver sponsors
  • Booth at the venue (first come, first serve )
  • 2 corporate tickets
  • Half-page ad in the conference brochure

Bronze sponsor

  • 1 corporate ticket
  • Mention with logotype in conference brochure

Other sponsorship options

If you feel that none of the above options suits you or you want to contribute more, we have other options to sponsor our conference. We are always looking for sponsorships to individual parts of the conference, like dinner, lunch or coffee breaks. In addition we welcom travel grants if you want to support people, who can't afford to travel, to attend our conference. If any of these options sounds good or you want to discuss about other options, don't hesitate to contact us.
